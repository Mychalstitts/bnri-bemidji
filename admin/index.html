Talking Points

Concierge Scripts

Short, memorable scripts the Concierge can adapt on a first call. Stay confident, stay specific, never overpromise.

Opening — any persona

"Thanks for reaching out about RenewMyBlock. I'm the BNRI Concierge — my job is to make sure you walk away with every benefit you're entitled to and a clean path through the city. Mind if I ask three quick questions so I can pull the right packet for you?"

Contractor

"You're going to deal with the city building department either way. The difference with BNRI is we pre-position your packet — fee waivers calculated, plan review pre-coordinated, and if you use one of our 48 pre-approved plans we cut the review time substantially."

"What we need from you: address, scope, and whether you've already pulled a Certificate of Survey. I'll handle the rest."

Homeowner — building own

"Three things stack on top of each other: the BNRI ~65% city fee waiver, a ten-year tax abatement on the improvement value, and a $10,000 grant. If you qualify for MN Housing's Start Up or First-Generation programs, that stacks on top again."

"Most owner-builders get stuck on the permit portal. I'll walk you through it on a screenshare — twenty minutes."

Homeowner — relocating

"218 Relocate gives you up to $2,500 plus a year of Paul Bunyan gigabit internet and free coworking — but you have to apply within thirty days of your move. Let's get that started before anything else."

Investor / REIT

"To be straight with you — Beltrami County isn't a federal Opportunity Zone, so I can't pitch you OZ tax deferral. What I can show you is the BNRI ten-year tax abatement on improvement value, ~65% fee waivers, sub-3% financing, and a vetted contractor pool that's done this work before. On a five-parcel deal that math is competitive with most OZ plays."

"Send me your acquisition criteria and I'll come back with parcels that fit, ROI modeled with the BNRI stack applied."

Partner organization

"Your members are exactly who BNRI is built for. Here's how we make it easy on you: any contact you send our way, I personally take the first call — your member never has to navigate the program alone. Want me to send over a co-branded one-pager you can put in your next newsletter?"

Closing — any persona

"I'm going to send you a packet today with everything we just discussed, the next three steps, and the names of the people you'll be working with. If you get stuck on anything, you call me — not the city. That's what I'm here for."

Position Description

RenewMyBlock Concierge — Role

A working description of the Concierge position. Use this for hiring, contracting, or training.

Mission

Be the single point of contact for every person and organization that touches BNRI. Translate a confusing program into a clean experience. Maximize the benefit each contact receives. Keep them moving — through permits, through financing, through to closing.

Daily responsibilities

📥 Inbox triage

Review website contact forms, route by persona, log every contact.

📞 First calls

15-minute discovery calls with each new contact within 24 hours.

📦 Packet preparation

Persona-specific packet with checklist, forms, fee waiver letter, partner referrals.

🏛️ City liaison

Coordinate with Bemidji building dept and P&Z to keep applications moving.

🤝 Partner stewardship

Monthly touch with each of the 14 partner orgs; co-marketing, MOUs, referrals.

📊 Reporting

Weekly metrics to Mychal: contacts, conversions to permit pulled, dollars committed.
