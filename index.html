Bemidji Neighborhood Renewal Incentive

A Transformative Partnership for a Thriving Bemidji

Launched May 13, 2025, BNRI incentivizes the remodel of 2,500 homes in targeted zones (starting 5th–15th Street west of Urban Avenue). By offering fee waivers, 10-year tax abatements, 5% grants (up to $10,000), and 2–3% financing, the program creates mixed-income neighborhoods, attracts professionals from Sanford Health and Bemidji State University, and drives $300–$500 million in economic impact by 2040.

Every stakeholder benefits: homeowners save thousands, builders gain demand, downtown businesses see $3.75–$7M annual revenue, and the city recoups costs within ~2 years post-abatement.
